The Worldwide Music Coding Session - BeatDJ Multiplayer

November 16, 2025 Jude Kerr
coding DAW music research

BeatDJ is a live-coding DAW created by Nes Croft and their company Soniare. It's a program made for microtonal improvised music that puts you into the cockpit and use the command line interface. It's a program made for the live performer, it's incredibly innovative in the world of DAWs in my own opinion and it's a super interesting project.

In this article, we're focusing on the efforts of the community in the world of multiplayer in BeatDJ.
